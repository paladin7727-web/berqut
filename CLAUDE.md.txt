## INTERACTIVITY (built and verified)
- **Intro**: full-screen video plays once/session, logo becomes clickable trigger 
  on completion; static branded fallback for prefers-reduced-motion/saveData
- **Background**: interactive line-art (currently a skyline-style motif ‚Äî client's 
  developer/designer may swap for an eagle-wing organic motif later), subtle mouse 
  parallax + scroll drift
- **CursorGlow**: soft gold light follows cursor, desktop only (hover:hover + 
  pointer:fine gate ‚Äî correctly disabled on touch devices, this is intentional)
- **TiltCard**: 3D tilt-toward-cursor on the 3 pillar cards + 3 service description 
  cards, with gold glare tracking
- **ProcessSteps**: FIND‚ÜíVERIFY‚ÜíDEAL‚ÜíGROW is clickable/hoverable, reveals detail 
  copy per stage (translated in all 7 languages)
- All interactive effects respect prefers-reduced-motion and gate off touch devices 
  appropriately; zero continuous animation loops (verified 0 idle requestAnimationFrame)
